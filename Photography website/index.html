PHOTOGRAPHERS

Ansel Adams

Ansel Easton Adams was an American landscape photographer and environmentalist known for his black-and-white images of the American West. He helped found Group f/64, an association of photographers advocating "pure" photography which favored sharp focus and the use of the full tonal range of a photograph


Cristina Mittermeier

Cristina Goettsch Mittermeier is a Mexican photographer, conservationist, biologist, and author. Cristina Mittermeier is a marine biologist and activist who pioneered the concept and field of conservation photography.


Annie Leibovitz

Anna-Lou Leibovitz is an American portrait photographer best known for her portraits, particularly of celebrities, which often feature subjects in intimate settings and poses.

Animators around the world

Ana Tortos

Ana is an art director, illustrator and animator based in Paris. With a charmingly surreal flair to her work, she works on music videos.


Genndy Tartakovsky

Gennady Borisovich "Genndy" Tartakovsky is a Russian-American animator, writer, producer, and director.


Ian Moo Young

Ian Moo-Young is an animator and graphic design artist. His talents were noticed at an early age in Kingston Jamaica and he received a scholarship from the British Council to study at Central Saint Martins College of Art & Design. Moo-Young has enjoyed a long and varied career.
